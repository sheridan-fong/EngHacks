About Us
Designed by Students to help Students

Our goal is to help students and young professionals perfect their interviewing skills. Our interview buddy asks you practical interview questions, and our machine learning process will analyze your speech patterns, pace, body language and the content of your responses. Your response will be graded based off of different criteria and will give you a score. Our goal is to give students practical interview experience.
