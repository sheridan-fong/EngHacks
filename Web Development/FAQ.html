FAQ

Why was this program created?
This program was created for students by students. Our goal is to help you perfect your interview skills so you can land the job of your dreams.
Where does the video go after I do a mock interview?
Your interview video is analyzed by a machine learning process. After analyzation your video is permenantly deleted from the server.
I don't see an interview deck that interests me... how do I suggest one?
If you feel like we're missing an interview deck that could benefit you and other students please submit the feedback form below. Include any necessary information you think is important such as the position and even the job description
How early should I be preparing for an interview?
It's never to early or to late to start preparing for an interview! You've made the right choice by coming to interview buddy. We suggest practicing for an interview as far ahead in advance as possible. There's never a wrong time to start practicing.
Who are the creators of this platform?
This platform was created by four biomedical engineers from McMaster University: Benjamin Sun, Ishan Vermani, Sharon Cai and Sheridan Fong
